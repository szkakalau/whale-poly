## 3. Why visualization matters

**Polymarket** markets are **high-dimensional**: outcomes, liquidity, time-to-resolution, and wallet behavior all interact.

Visualization helps you:

- **Spot outliers** without mistaking noise for “alpha”

- **Move faster** from “interesting” → “verify rules” → “size risk”

Dashboards do not remove the need for **resolution literacy**—they reduce search cost.

## 6. Risks and limitations

- **Metric confusion:** “leaderboard” can mislead if you do not know the **window** and **sample**.  
- **Survivorship:** top rows may reflect regime luck—**Smart Money** is a **prior**, not proof.  
- **Latency:** dashboards can lag the fastest participants.  
- **UI bias:** pretty charts can increase **overconfidence**.
